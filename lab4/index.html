Abstract:

The prominent aspects of technological progress include current technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), computer vision (CV), and the Internet of Things (IoT) that apply to many sectors, particularly surveillance cameras, which use facial recognition in real time to enhance security. In general, security robots are the most prominent security systems that assist humans in dangerous jobs that humans cannot do. Herein, a low-cost robot prototype running on Raspberry PI 4 model B is proposed for enhanced safety and smart security. This smart robot functions to detect whether an intruder has come inside with the help of IoT and face recognition technology. The broadcasting of photos can take place with the room control through IoT. The system utilizes a facial recognition algorithm, separating authorized persons from intruders. The Multi-task Cascaded Convolutional Neural Network (MTCNN) algorithm is employed in image processing to identify and classify people with an accuracy of 90%. The IoT will notify the room control system if a stranger is detected. An interface is designed to control the robot, which is linked to Wi-Fi from a far end. If the person is recognized as a known person, then authorization is provided to them.

1.Introduction

today's fast-changing technological world, the fusion of AI and robotics has transformed how security and safety are approached. Facial recognition technology emerges as a standout innovation, offering real-time identification capabilities. When integrated with robotics, it covers the way for delicate safety and security across various domains, especially in industrial and commercial sectors. The potential of facial recognition robots as an advanced solution for strengthening safety protocols and implementing intelligent security measures is discussed by merging advanced facial recognition with the agility and adaptability of robots. Organizations can achieve greater situational awareness, proactive threat detection, and swift response mechanisms .

A robot is a machine that has computer programming which is capable of performing an intricate series of tasks on its own. Modern industrial automation and monitoring systems heavily rely on robots. Robots have become more useful and have more applications as technology has advanced. When people and working robots collaborate, their jobs will become easier and less complex [3]. With computer technology and various vision devices, including cameras and 3D scans, it can recognize and process digital photographs of human faces. Facial recognition technology has emerged as an interesting field of study for robotic systems, smart homes and cities, and security applications.

IoT has recently entered the surveillance space to improve security and provide personal safety. 2.Related work

Biometric identification and security surveillance are two sectors that heavily rely on face recognition technologies. Yet it's evident that mobile robots, such as humanoid robots or security surveillance robots, will finally need to transition from traditional fixed position to mobile environment recognition. This study therefore sought to evaluate facial recognition systems' performance in both stationary and mobile settings.

Face recognition technology was first utilized a few years ago for restricted personal identification. Since then, its usage in security has expanded to include preventing fraud, criminal activity, and terrorist attacks. Furthermore, facial recognition is utilized in marketing, advertising, health, and other fields in addition to determining a driver's level of fatigue and lowering the number of collisions on the road. Numerous techniques are created to provide the highest level of face recognition accuracy. The field has adopted the deep learning approach due to its remarkable computing speed and resultant surprising results.

There are many previous approaches where the focus was important on providing security but not on the efficiency of the security system.   1. The robot can be developed in such a way that it provides surveillance and security, which ensures a smarter way of providing security.
  2. A Raspberry Pi camera can detect and recognize humans based on facial features
  3. The goal of this system could be to create a facial recognition-based access control system that improves security and simplifies access control in a building or organization.
  4. Using a Raspberry Pi module reduces power consumption, makes it more power efficient, and saves costs.

Whitespace was strategically used to avoid overcrowding, allowing the content to breathe and improving legibility. Padding was applied to elements like buttons and containers to make them more clickable and comfortable, especially for mobile devices.

Buttons and links were designed to be visually distinct. Larger buttons with hover effects (like subtle color transitions) improve the user experience by making it clear when elements are interactive. Rounded corners add a modern touch, enhancing the site's aesthetic.

Animations and transitions were used sparingly, adding polish without detracting from the content. Simple hover transitions, such as fading button colors, help create a smooth and responsive experience for the user. The timing is quick enough to be noticeable without being distracting.

Accessibility was a key consideration in the design. High-contrast text and background colors were used to ensure readability for all users, including those with visual impairments.
